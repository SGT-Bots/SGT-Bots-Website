Privacy Policy

SGT Bots

Last Update: 07/10/23 | Effective Date: 10/10/23

Overview

Thanks for using SGT Bots Products, We care about your privacy and want to be upfront about how we treat your personal data: what we do collect, why we collect it, where it all goes, and for how long.
If you have any questions or suggestions, write us at support@sgtbots.com.

Important Definitions

The following are terms that are frequently referred to in this privacy policy but not common in everyday language. We wanted to take a bit of extra time to explain them.

  • Personal Data means any information that identifies or relates to a particular individual (often referred to as “personally identifiable information” or “PII”). Data that is completely anonymized and cannot be linked with an individual user is NOT considered Personal Data.

  • Service Providers help us run our services. They may include: Hosting, database, and communication providers, such as the authentication service we use to allow you to log into your account.

What personal data do we collect and how do we collect it?

Category of Personal Data Example When we collect this data What do we use this data for
Profile Data Username, Email, Hashed and Salted Password Directly from you, when you choose to give it to us (such as when you sign up for an SGT account) -So that we can make the service work
-So that we can send you emails for Account Activity (E.g. Password reset, Email change)

When you visit our websites

We do NOT use cookies to track visitors to our website. The requests made to the web server are kept for logging purposes.

When you choose to use certain products

SGT Accounts. When you create an SGT Account your data is stored with our Service Provider so that you can access it on other devices. We do not use or access this data for anything other than displaying your content to you and to deliver our services.

TheShadowMusic. When you use the TheShadowMusic Discord bot (TSM, bot) all command usage and playback data is logged anonymously, to provide some services your query may be sent to some third-party services (e.g. Youtube) anonymously, to use features that utilize SGT Account integration the bot may communicate with our SGT Account database service provider to retrieve information necessary for the use of the feature

Under what conditions would we share your personal data?

We never sell your data, and we’ll only share it in the following circumstances:

  • With our Service Providers to help us run our services. They may include: Hosting, database, and communication providers, such as the authentication service we use to allow you to log into your account.

  • If you ask us to. We’ll share your data when you directly authorize it to be shared or share it yourself.

How do we store and protect your data?

We do our best to protect the Personal Data we collect from any unauthorized parties accessing, using, or sharing it, but it’s important to remember that no method of transmitting data over the internet or storing data is 100% secure. We also ask you to help us out with protecting your data. Easy steps include creating a strong password and limiting access to your device and account credentials.

We hold on to Personal Data for as long as you have an account with us or as otherwise necessary to provide you with our Services. We’ll only hang on to it for longer if needed to meet our legal requirements discussed above, or if it’s anonymized so that it can’t be linked to a specific user.

If you are a resident in the EU you can request to have: Access, Rectification, Restrict processing (where possible), object to processing and request a copy of your data. If you would like to exercise this right contact us at support@sgtbots.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We’re constantly trying to improve our product and services, so we may need to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make big changes, we’ll let you know by placing a notice on our Website, however even if you do not receive any notice the updated policy will still apply.
